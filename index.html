Everything you need

Powerful features for every role

From solo practice at home to managing a multi-branch institute, Music Journey has it covered.

Practice Tracking

Log practice sessions with timers, attach audio or video recordings, and build consistent daily habits.

Goals & Targets

Set practice goals with deadlines. Teachers assign targets and verify completion in-session.

Student Rankings

Teachers verify practiced items in-session, awarding scores. A transparent ranking motivates consistent progress.

Scheduling

Manage lesson schedules, teacher availability, and get automatic conflict detection across the institute.

Payments & Wallets

Integrated payments for course enrollments. Institutes configure teacher revenue share per session.

Smart Notifications

Push notifications, in-app alerts, and email — triggered at the right moment for lessons, payments, and updates.

Multi-Branch Support

Manage multiple locations from a single account. Assign teachers and students per branch.

Course Catalog

Offer private, group, or workshop courses. Students browse and enroll directly from within the app.

Teacher Hiring

Institutes post open positions. Teachers browse a map of hiring schools and apply directly in-app.
